Perception Drift and Lips

In aesthetic medicine, lips are often the focal point of enhancement...

✅ How to Prevent Overfilled Lips:

  • ✅ Use before-and-after photos to track changes over time.
  • ✅ Encourage rest periods between treatments.
  • ✅ Have honest conversations about what looks balanced and natural.
  • ✅ Most importantly: Educate patients and ourselves.
“We have a duty of care to recognize Perception Drift — in our patients, and in ourselves.”
